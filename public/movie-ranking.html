Recommendations for a movie night by Wei

Top 3 Movies for various genres

Animation

Spirited Away

All time classic. Beautiful music accompanied with the most beautiful scenery. One of the best animation done by Studio Ghibli

Garden of Words

One of my personal favourites. Also a very short film with only 40 minutes of watch time. The story and animation are very captivating.

Ponyo

Another Studio Ghibli movie, but with its own very unique charm. Always a pleasure to rewatch, you can't do anything wrong with it.
